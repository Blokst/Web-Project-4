What is Naawi-Oodena

.

Naawi-Oodena is an urban reserve plan in the heart of Winnipeg, Manitoba.
The former military base is being transformed into a vibran, invlusive and susstainable community led by the Treaty One Developemt in partenrship with Canada Lands Company

The 160 acre area will be used for

  • This 160-acre redevelopment will
  • Celebrate First Nations culture and identity
  • Create thousands of new homes and jobs
  • Feature Indigenous architecture
  • Offer parks, schools, cultural centres, and recreation spaces
  • Advance reconciliation and community connection
